Hello there👋, I am Luigi Sigillo.

🧪 I am a **Postdoctoral Fellow** at the [EMBL (European Molecular Biology Laboratory)](https://www.embl.org/), working with the [Fabio Petroni](https://www.fabiopetroni.com/) group. We build AI solutions that combine **multimodal reasoning**, **agentic exploration**, **tool usage**, **knowledge retrieval**, and **lab-in-the-loop interaction** to accelerate scientific discovery.
